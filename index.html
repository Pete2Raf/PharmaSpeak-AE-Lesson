Listening: Reporting an Adverse Event

Comprehension

  1. What reaction did the patient experience?
  2. When did the rash appear?
  3. What action did the doctor take?
  4. How long did it take for the rash to clear?
  5. What information did the medical department ask for?
  6. What did the rep promise to do at the end of the call?
